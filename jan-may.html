What's included

Built for
development.

  • Weekly training led by experienced coaches
  • Technical work — dribbling, turns, passing, receiving, shooting
  • Small-sided games (3v3, 4v4) for maximum touches
  • Tactics — positional roles (Fixo, Ala, Pivo), rotations, set pieces
  • Skill-based groupings — players are placed by age, experience, and ability
