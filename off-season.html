Between seasons

The Off-Season.

Our two main seasons run Jan–May and Aug–Nov. December, June, and July fall in between — instead of our regular weekly schedule, we run Summer Camps on select dates.

Contact Us for Camp Dates
What the off-season looks like

Camp-based,
not weekly sessions.

We don't run our normal weekly training schedule during the off-season. Instead, we offer Summer Camps on select dates — focused sessions that keep players sharp and give them extra reps outside the regular season.

December, June, and July are also peak vacation and family time, so camps are scheduled around specific dates rather than a weekly commitment. Contact us for the current camp calendar.

A few things to know

Why it's a bit
less predictable.

✓
Camps, not weekly sessions

Off-season training runs as Summer Camps on select dates rather than our regular weekly schedule.

✓
No weekly commitment

There's no ongoing attendance required during the off-season — just join us for the camp dates that work for your family.

+
Coaches may take breaks

Camps are scheduled around coach availability, so there may be stretches with no camps at all.

+
Dates aren't fixed

This is why our next season's start date isn't set in stone until it's close — camp and off-season timing shifts year to year.
