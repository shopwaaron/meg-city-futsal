Our mission

Building complete
players in SoCal.

Our mission is to provide youth soccer players in Southern California with an exciting and challenging futsal program that opens doors to compete in regional, national, and international competition. Our goal is to give young players the proper training and exposure to increase their opportunities in both futsal and soccer.

Meg City Futsal is a year-round futsal training program built to help players reach their full potential and experience the highest levels of competition. We make every effort to develop a better overall soccer player through futsal. Players who consistently attend and show development may be invited to represent the club in competitive settings.

Our values

Three things
we hold to.

Respect

For coaches, referees, parents, teammates, and opponents. Always and without exception.

Kindness

The youth futsal and soccer community is competitive, but we are all connected and work together.

Fun

Experience is key. We hope every player enjoys the game and stays in it for life.
